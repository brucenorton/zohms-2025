TECHNICAL SUPPORT

Feature

  • Zero Ohm functionality
  • No transformers required
  • Multiple loudspeakers can be connected in parallel
  • Any speaker impedance can be used: 2/4/8/16 Ohm
  • Uniform power distribution over long distance cable runs
  • Full range frequency response

    • Technical Overview

    Up until now, the ability to connect multiple speakers in parallel was confronted by the issue of operating the amplifier at a safe nominal wattage. Although there are amplifiers that operate at 4 ohms and 2 ohms safely, any effort to drive it below the rated resistance led to device failures.
    With this revolutionary invention, the rules have changed!
